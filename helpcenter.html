When do i get my order?

For standard orders, please allow up to:

  • 2 working days for production and despatch of your item, from the date of placing your order (you should receive an automated email when your product has been despatched from the factory);
  • 2 working days from date of despatch for delivery to the United Kingdom (items are sent via Royal Mail first class post);
  • 3-5 working days from date of despatch for delivery to the European Union countries;
  • 5-7 working days from date of despatch for delivery to the United States;
  • 7 working days from date of despatch for delivery to other countries.

For orders placed with trackable option, please allow up to:

  • 2 working days for production and despatch of your item, from the date of placing your order (you should receive an automated email when your products has been despatched from the factory);
  • 1 working day from date of despatched for delivery to the United Kingdom;
  • 2-4 working days from date despatched for delivery to the European Union countries;
  • 4-6 working days from date despatched for delivery to the United States;
  • 6 working days from date despatched for delivery to other countries (for some countries tracking may not apply, please check before placing your order).

My order is late!

Keep calm, we’ll have your order by the time you say ‘governmental accountability’! We send our items via Royal Mail AirSure and follow their standard delivery times, but occasional delays may occur.

If your order is not received by the due delivery date, we replace the item free of charge. You can contact our friendly helpdesk team to let them know your order is late at (help@keepcalm-o-matic.co.uk)

What is my tracking number?

Tracking numbers will be supplied on request for users who have selected the ‘trackable’ order option when purchasing. Contact our support team for more info (help@keepcalm-o-matic.co.uk)

How to make sure your gift arrives on time:

We know it’s important to you that gift arrive on time for the special events in your life, be it birthday, Christmas time or Easter season.

We can guarantee delivery of your gift before Christmas this year if you place your order:

  • Before the 18th of December for United Kingdom deliveries
  • Before the 16th December for European Union states deliveries
  • Before the 12th of December for USA or rest of the world deliveries

Can i change the custom design on my order?

The shade of blue isn’t quite right? If you absolutely must change the design on your custom item, please get in touch with our support team. If you email us the next working day of placing your order, we might be able to help, contact our friendly customer support team at help@keepcalm-o-matic.co.uk

Please make sure the image is a proper resolution for printing before placing your order (an image of at least 2000x2000 pixels is reccommended). To note if the image looks bad on your computer screen (looks pixelated or has missing details), there’s a high chance it will look bad when printing.

I made a mistake on my delivery address

Delivery address is very important to ensure we get your order to destination in due time. Be sure to add your:

  • House number
  • ZIP code
  • town, province and country

Typos, a missing house number or just a wrong ZIP code can easily get your order lost in the mail system. Please contact our support team within 24 hours of placing your order if you notice that you made a mistake on your address. The sooner the better! (help@keepcalm-o-matic.co.uk)

Orders with incorrect or incomplete delivery address are generally returned to sender within two weeks time. We will re-ship your order if we have a correct address for you.

Can i cancel my order?

Seriously, you want to cancel your order? Alright… we can cancel your order if:

  • You request cancelling the next working day after placing your order, and your order has not already been despatched by our printing department;
  • You ordered the same item by mistake twice;
  • There is an unforseen inability to produce the goods (;

Can i get a refund for my payment?

We can refund your payment if:

  • You received a damaged product;
  • Your received a different product than what you ordered;
  • Your order was not delivered;
  • Your order is 48 hours late of the due delivery date;
  • You return the products within 14 days of delivery date;

Please get in touch with us if you have such trouble (help@keepcalm-o-matic.co.uk)

How do i remove an image?

To remove an image from our website, you can:

  • Report the poster using the ‘report’ feature on the poster page – our staff will review your request and take the image down if needed
  • Contact our friendly customer support team at help@keepcalm-o-matic.co.uk

How do i create a private poster?

Public posters can be created by anyone who visits our website. They are, just as the name states, public: they will be shown to everyone who explores our Gallery of images, and anyone can find them easily on a Google search

To create a private poster, just:

  • Log in to your Keep Calm-o-Matic account
  • Create your image
  • Tick the ‘private’ box before generating the image

Private posters will not be seen by anyone but the author of the poster (visible in the account page) and the users who have the link to the poster.

This poster is offensive/i'm being bullied

You can find a button to report a poster on every poster page on our website.

Reported posters are reviewed by our staff and removed if they don’t comply with our content policy.

You should always use the report option if you see a poster in the categories below:

  • Highly inappropriate and offensive content, images or text
  • Contains messages that incite hate or violence
  • Contains racial, ethnic or sexist slurs
  • Is used for bullying or harassing someone

We are NOT OK with bullying or any messages that incite violence and will investigate any reports concerning activities of illegal nature.

How to create my poster? It’s as easy as 1-2-3, just (use our software and:

  1. Change the text and customize font, colour, style
  2. Choose the logo
  3. Choose your background image

Any posters that are public when created on your website will be public (displayed in our Gallery for the whole world to see). To make your poster private, you need to be logged in with your account and tick the ‘private’ box before generating the image.
